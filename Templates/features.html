FEATURES

Click To Eat Delivery is a very friendly application for everybody and it is very easy to use. We have a customer service call on our app that can assist the blind people on their order.

The Click to Eat group is the leading global online food delivery marketplace. Headquartered in Cebu City, Philippines.

Click to Eat allows users to conveniently discover food around their neighbourhood and directly order their favourite meals online or via mobile.

Click to Eat was launched in June 2021.

We are a small team of problem solvers, designers, thinkers and tinkers, working around the clock to make click to eat the most powerful online tool for food delivery. We believe that ordering food should be easy, fast and definitely fun!
