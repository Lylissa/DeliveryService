PRIVACY POLICY

This policy explains how we, clicktoeat, a brand of http://www.clicktoeat.com, utilize the personal information you give us when you use our services, such as our website and mobile applications (apps).

What information we collect about you?

When you order goods or services from us or use our website, we collect personal information from you. When you complete a customer survey, we collect information as well. Cookies can also be used to collect information about how people use a website.

Cookies and Google Analytics

Cookies are little text files that websites place on your computer when you visit them. They're commonly employed to make websites work or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to site owners.

Google Analytics, a web analytics tool offered by Google, Inc., is used on this page ("Google"). To help the website assess how you use the site, Google Analytics employs "cookies," which are text files saved on your computer. Google will transfer and store the information produced by the cookie regarding your use of the website on servers in the United States. If this website anonymizes IP addresses, Google will truncate your IP address within an EU member state or another EEA state before sending it to the United States. Your complete IP address will only be communicated to Google servers in the United States and truncated there in rare circumstances. Google will use this information to evaluate your website usage, compile website activity reports for website operators, and provide other services related to website activity and internet usage. Google will not link your IP address to any other information it has. You can disable cookies in your browser by changing the necessary settings, but please keep in mind that you may not be able to enjoy all of the features of this website if you do so. By using this website, you agree to Google using your personal data in the way and for the reasons described above. You can also prevent Google from collecting and processing information (including your IP address) via cookies by downloading and installing this browser plugin: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Most online browsers provide some level of cookie control via the browser settings. Visit www.allaboutcookies.org to learn more about cookies, including how to discover what cookies have been placed and how to manage and delete them.

How will we use the information we collect from you?

We use the information you provide to execute your order and manage your account. We may also use your information to send you emails about other products or services we think you'd like.

We may send your information to credit reference and fraud prevention services in order to process your order.

We may send your information to third parties who we believe may have goods or services that are of interest to you from time to time. Please email us at support@foodpanda.ph if you do not want to be contacted by third parties.

Access to your information

At any time, you have the right to seek a copy of the information we have on you. If you'd like a copy of this information, please send an email to support@clicktoeat.ph. This request will incur a modest fee for processing.
